2023.05.31. PM 4:41

The Boston Celtics were expected to win three consecutive games after three consecutive losses and make a historic record in Game 7 at home, but the NBA record dominated the Miami Heat as the winner. In 151 NBA playoffs history, no team has won four consecutive games after three losses. Miami, in particular, repaid its revenge for losing to the Celtics in the Eastern Conference final last year with three wins and four losses. This is the seventh time that they have advanced to the finals since 2006. It is the highest number in the NBA during this period.

Boston is a three-point shooting team. When the success rate of three-point shots exceeded 40%, the winning rate was guaranteed with 49 wins and 2 losses over the past two seasons, but when it fell below, it was not half-strike with 29 wins and 33 losses. The biggest reason why he failed to take advantage of the home court and was completely defeated by the Heat 103-84 was also due to the failure of a three-point shot. He shot 42 three-point shots and had a 21% success rate with just nine.

There were only four cases that led to Game 7 after losing three games in PO history, including this Boston. Boston is the first to extend the game to home after three consecutive Illimination games on the cliff. In fact, Boston fans expected the Celtics to make a new history as the Red Sox of the same franchise made the myth of winning four consecutive games after the only three losses in the Major League in 2004. However, the previous 150 failed records failed in front of the Celtics.

The Denver Nuggets, who were confirmed to advance to the finals early as the Western Conference champion due to the bloody match between the Celtics and the Heat, are much more likely to win. Some cautiously predict that the 2023 final may end with a short series.

Denver is a perfect team for offense and defense. It is ahead of Miami in under-goal, outside, bench, and defense. Denver, which advanced to the final for the first time in the club's history, has high-sensitivity outer shots by center Nikola Yogic (28) and "Nuggetspan Stephon Curry" Jamal Murray (26). Yogic has a triple-double record in 15 playoff games. It is 27.8 points per game, 14.5 rebounds, and 11.8 assists.

Moreover, power is superior even under equal conditions, but Miami has consumed a lot of physical strength in Game 7, so it is advantageous. Miami also reached the final with the lowest score (109.5 points) in the regular season. Miami is the third team to advance to the finals of the lowest scoring team in the regular season.

Miami, which has won three NBA titles in its career, is the second team to advance to the NBA Finals as the eighth seed after being seeded in the playoffs in 1984. The New York Knicks were the first team in 1999. The Eastern Conference Knicks advanced to the final, beating the Miami Heat, Atlanta Hawks, and Indiana Pacers one after another. At this time, the first round of the conference was the best-of-five series. However, they lost to the San Antonio Spurs of David Robinson, Tim Duncan, Sean Elliot and Steve Kerr (now Golden State Warriors) 4-1 in the final. History is likely to repeat itself again this time.

The 2023 NBA Finals will begin on June 2. The format is 2-2-1-1-1. Denver, which has a high winning rate in the regular season, is home.
